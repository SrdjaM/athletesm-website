About Me

This is my story!

My love for sports has guided me since I was a child. The first great loves were karate and basketball. During high school I developed a desire for gym and martial arts such as: Sanda Sansho, Wing Chun and MMA. The desire for a blog arose in the middle of a pandemic. I combined my two great loves, a love for writing and a love for sports, and AthleteSM was born!
